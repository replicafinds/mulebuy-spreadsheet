Shipping Outerwear from MuleBuy — Weight, Cost and Packaging Tips

2026-04-10 路 replicafind.com
鈫?All Guides

Outerwear is the most expensive category to ship because of its weight and bulk. Getting consolidation and shipping route selection right can save $40-80 per haul. Here is everything you need to know.

Why Outerwear Shipping Is Different

A puffer jacket weighs 600-900 grams. A Canada Goose-style parka can weigh over a kilogram. When you add multiple pieces of outerwear to a haul, the weight adds up fast. MuleBuy charges shipping by dimensional weight (a formula that accounts for both physical weight and package volume), so a bulky-but-light down jacket can cost more to ship than a heavier-but-denser item.

The key is consolidation. Shipping three jackets together is not three times the cost of shipping one — it is closer to 1.5-2x the cost, because the base shipping fee is amortized across all items.

Compressing Before Shipping

MuleBuy can compress down jackets before packaging — compressing the fill significantly reduces dimensional weight and therefore shipping cost. Ask for compression in your shipping request. Well-compressed puffers can save 20-30% on shipping costs with no lasting effect on the jacket (down fill re-lofts after unpacking).

Note: compression works for down fill. Structured jackets (Canada Goose parkas, Carhartt Detroit jackets) cannot be compressed meaningfully without damaging the structure.

Route Selection

For outerwear shipments, the mid-tier shipping routes offer the best balance of cost and customs handling. The cheapest routes sometimes have stricter customs inspections for bulky packages. Premium express is fast but expensive for heavy shipments.

Mixing Outerwear with Lighter Items

The most efficient hauls mix heavy outerwear with lighter items (sneakers, polos, accessories). The lighter items add relatively little to shipping weight while spreading the base shipping cost across more pieces.

For example: two puffer jackets plus four polos and a pair of sneakers ships for roughly the same total shipping cost as two puffer jackets alone — making the effective per-piece shipping cost significantly lower.
